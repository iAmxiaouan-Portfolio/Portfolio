Lexus_Vision Coupe

Automotive Design / 2026

A vision for the next-generation grand tourer — where autonomous driving liberates the interior architecture from conventional constraints. The Horizon reimagines the relationship between driver, passenger, and the road ahead.

The exterior language draws from tension between aerodynamic necessity and sculptural ambition. Each surface is resolved to carry light in a way that shifts with the viewing angle, creating a form that feels alive even when still.
