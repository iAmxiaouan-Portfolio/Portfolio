Void Architecture

Interior Concept / Spatial Design / 2023

What happens when the interior of a vehicle becomes the primary design object? Void Architecture removes the dashboard, the center console, the familiar landmarks — and starts again from the negative space.

The cabin is conceived as a single continuous volume. Surfaces flow without interruption. Controls emerge from material itself, appearing only when summoned. The goal: an interior that feels less like a cockpit and more like a room that moves.
