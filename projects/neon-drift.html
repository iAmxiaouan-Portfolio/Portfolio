Neon Drift Study

Lighting & Material Exploration / 2023

An investigation into how light defines automotive surfaces after dark. Using long-exposure photography and digital rendering, this study captures the interplay between artificial light sources and complex compound curves.

The work reveals how a single surface can hold multiple identities depending on the light that falls upon it — warm sodium vapor versus cold LED, focused spot versus diffused ambient.
