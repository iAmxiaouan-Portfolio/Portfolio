2045 Silent Form

Luxury Sedan / Form Study / 2024

Silent Form is a meditation on restraint. In a world saturated with aggressive design language, this project asks: can a car command presence through quietness alone?

Every crease is purposeful. The greenhouse tapers with geometric precision, while the lower body carries a single, unbroken horizon line from headlamp to tail. There is nothing to add, nothing to remove.
