Urban Fragments

Photography Series / Street / 2023

Cities are machines for living, and their surfaces bear the marks of that use. Urban Fragments collects the textures, geometries, and accidental compositions found in everyday infrastructure — the poetry hidden in concrete, steel, and glass.
