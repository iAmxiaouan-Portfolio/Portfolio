2026 E-VTOL_Flux X

E-VTOL Concpet Design / 2026

Ethereal Motion explores the visual language of speed without combustion — what does velocity look like when it's silent? The project strips away conventional performance cues to find a new vocabulary for electric sportscars.

The body dissolves into air at its edges. Surfaces are thinned to impossible sharpness, suggesting a machine that exists at the threshold between solid and atmosphere.
